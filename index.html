About Us



The Pastor's Seeds Family of the Redeemed Christian Church of God is the fellowship of the
seeds (children) of Pastors and Ministers in the Redeemed Christian Church of God. This does
not limit our reach as we welcome the association with other Bible believing ministries.

The Pastors Seed Family(PSF) of the Redeemed Christian Church of God is a body of Pastors,
Ministers and Workers chhildren (seeds) between the ages 13-30 years. They are driven by the
vision of living a godly lifestyle for other young adults to emulate.

Region 5 consists of all seeds in Rivers State, Nigeria.

Seed Up Strong



Seed up Strong is the official monthly newsletter of the Pastors’ Seed
Family in Region 5.

Initially, the thoughts behind the newsletter was just to give us more
creative and easy way to report all our regional activities in the month at
once.

However, Seed Up Strong is growing daily with the inclusion of Seed of the
Month awards, birthday and anniversary celebrations, personal editorial
columns, adverts for seeds in business, and so much more.
